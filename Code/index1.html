  • Electronic Money Order

    eMO is a web based rapid money transfer service offered by India Post between two individuals within India.

  • Philately

    It gives information about stamps,Guidelines for Use of Images of Postage Stamps,list of all Philately Offices.

  • Information

    It gives Information about Pincode search,Track and Trace of speed post

  • Pickup service

    customer can ask the postman to pickup Packets of different sizes from their home,cost of this service depends on size of the Packet.

  • Address Book

    User can Register their address,update their current address when there is a transfer so that post to the old address will be redirected to their current address automatically. users can also search others addresses

  • What is ePostOffice?

    The e-Post Office is the shopping portal of the postal service on the internet.It provides services like eMO,Philately,Address search and gives useful information about the post office.

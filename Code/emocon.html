eMO BOOKING PROCEDURE

login click on Book Now

Remitter name will come automatically .

Fill the details of “Pay to”

Select the standard message like Money for payment of loan etc.

Select Payment mode either Debit card or Credit card.

At present only Axis Bank Cards are accepted.

Click on Confirm Payment.

The page will redirect to Banks payment page.

Enter the Card Details.

eMO will be booked after successful payment.

eMO DELIVERY PROCEDURE

eMOs payable at post offices in account with printing HO / SO will be transferred along with two copies of list under receipt obtained on the 3rd copy of the list to Sub-account Assistant for dispatching the same to the post office duly entered in SO / BO slip. These can be transmitted through Account Bag or through Registered Bag entering the list of eMOs in registered list to such suboffices if there is a delivery advantage.

FAQ ON eMO

1. What is eMO?

eMO is a web based rapid money transfer service offered by India Post between two individuals within India.

2. How fast is the money transfer?

Money booked through eMO can be disbursed within 24 hours.

3. How much money can be sent through eMO?

A minimum of Rs.1/- and a maximum of Rs. 5000./- can be sent through eMO.
